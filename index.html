Frequently Asked Questions

Simply search for restaurants near you, browse their menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Choose your payment method and your order will be placed instantly.

Visit our Partner With Us page, fill in your restaurant details and submit the required documents. Our team will verify and onboard you within 48 hours.

Delivery charges vary based on distance and order value. Your first order is completely free! After that, we offer free delivery on orders above ₹299.

Orders can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placement for a full refund. After preparation has started, cancellations are subject to review. Contact support for assistance.
